American Style Buttermilk Pancakes Recipe:



    Ingredients:

  • 100g plain flour
  • 2tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 1tbsp golden caster sugar
  • 2 large eggs; separate the whites and yolk
  • 284ml Buttermilk
  • 25g butter, melted and extra butter (of course 😉)


    Method:

  1. Sift the flour into a bowl
  2. Add the baking powder, bicarbonate of soda, sugar and a pinch of salt into a bowl. Set the bowl aside
  3. Whisk the egg whites until stiff peaks form.
  4. Follow this up, by mixing the yolks, buttermilk and melted butter
  5. Whisk in the flour mix until batter thickens.
  6. Fold the egg whites into the batter until evenly mixed.
  7. Heat a large frying pan and add melted butter.
  8. Place 3 large spoons of batter into the pan giving you 10cm diameter pancakes.
  9. After 3 minutes, when bubbles start to form, flip the pancakes over and cook until browned.
  10. Then top with syrup, raspberries and berries.
