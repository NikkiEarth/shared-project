Vanilla Cake

Vanilla Cake Recipe:

Ingredients:

  • 200g caster sugar
  • 200g softened butter
  • 4 eggs, beaten
  • 200g self-raising flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • Vanilla Essence, 5 drops

Method:

  1. Pre-heat oven to 190C/fan 170C/gas 5.
  2. Butter a 20cm sandwich tin and line with non-stick baking paper.
  3. In a large bowl, beat 200g caster sugar, vanilla essence, 200g softened butter, 4 beaten eggs, 200g self-raising flour, 1 tsp baking powder and 2 tbsp milk together until you have a smooth, soft batter.
  4. Add the mixture in a baking tin and smooth the surface with a spatula or the back of a spoon.
  5. Bake for about 20 mins until golden and the cake springs back when pressed.
  6. Turn onto a cooling rack and leave to cool completely.
  7. Eat as you please!
